About me

Hello, my name is Nicole, I am 19 years old and was born on the 27th of august of 2004. I was born in London and lived there for a few years before moving to Sydney, where we lived for eight months before moving again to Singapore where we lived for another year before finally settling down in Madrid, Spain. Before marrying my mother my dad was married so I have two older half-sisters and a half-brother, and I also have an older sister and a younger brother. I grew up in Madrid where I initially started school in the British Council and then transferred to Hastings School when I was 7.

I am currently studying a Bachelors Degree in Creative Computing, a three year course in Programming with an outlook into the creative industry, helping incorporate AR, VR and XR into our current day-to-day apps while still thinking about the socio-economic and ethical effects of what we are doing which is important as this is one of the most revolutionary industries of the time. A-level and career choices

Growing up, I have always been a very curious person and have always felt the need to go beyond what the human-eye can see, which could explain my passion for chemistry and final choice of career. I have always had small seed of doubt as to why the world works how it does and why things are the way they are, from things so simple as to why the sky is blue to many questions on how complex some things we take for granted in our day to day life are. The latter is the main reason as to my final career choice, as I started learning how simple appliences such as microwaves, televisions or vacuum cleaners worked I started becoming more and more interested in how technology could make such a vast difference in our lives without most people not even realising it.

Some of my biggest passions and hobbies include: baking, photography and video edition and reading.
